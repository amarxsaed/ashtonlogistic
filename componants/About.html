About Ashton Logistics

Your trusted partner in the trucking industry, connecting carriers with premium loads nationwide since 2021.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Bensalem, Pennsylvania, Ashton Logistics was born out of a deep understanding of the trucking industry’s challenges. Our founders, who spent years working on the road, witnessed firsthand how traditional dispatch services burden carriers with high upfront fees, forced dispatch, and limited broker connections—leaving truckers with lower earnings and fewer opportunities.

Determined to change the status quo, we built Ashton Logistics on a simple yet powerful mission: to transform truck dispatching by putting carriers first. We pioneered a “pay after you get paid” model that removes financial risk from your journey, and we’ve invested heavily in forging relationships with over 200 trusted brokers nationwide.

With most of our team based right here in the US, we efficiently handle critical tasks like Highway and RMIS packets—ensuring that every requirement is met without compromise. Today, hundreds of carriers trust us to connect them with exclusive, high-paying loads, because we believe our success is measured by your success.
